Silent Night

Genius Or Fool?

For some reason, you decide that the best way to handle a situation where a stranger has intruded your home and is hiding in your living room, potentially with a weapon, is to walk boldly towards the person. As soon as you step upon the living room carpet, you are seen, by the stranger crouching behind your couch, who was trying to remain unseen. You put up very meager resistance as they stab the heck out of you.
